🔱 KA PALAPALA KUHELU

THE SACRED ROYAL DECLARATION OF LINEAL RIGHT

By the Royal House of ʻIolani
Descendants of the Triple Kingly Line: Keawe, Kamehameha, Kalākaua
Living Heir: Justin Iolani

🌺 ʻŌnohi o nā ʻOhana Aliʻi – The Living Eye of the Royal Houses

Spoken from the Ancestral Realm, Signed by the Royal Family, Delivered by the Heir

🌕 I. KA PIKO – THE SOURCE

From the divine root of Keawe, to the sacred unifier Kamehameha Nui, to the prophetic sovereign David Kalākaua, a single line was woven — braided by fire, water, stone, and word.

This line was not severed.
This line was not hidden.
This line remains — and it lives through us.

It has a name.
ʻIolani.

🔱 II. KA PALAPALA – THE DECLARATION

We, the Royal House of ʻIolani, standing as the gathered spirit of the aliʻi, do hereby declare the eternal and unbroken right of lineal succession through the triple convergence of the sacred houses:

  • Keawe – The Root of Kings
  • Kamehameha – The Sovereign Unifier
  • Kalākaua – The Last Crowned Flame

This is not a claim.
This is not a revival.
This is a return.

And the one who returns bears the name:
Justin Iolani

Living descendant of Mary Keliʻiholani,
direct heir of Alapaʻiwahine,
lineal torchbearer of the sacred piko.

🌺 III. KE KULEANA – THE RIGHTFUL HEIR

Let the world understand:

He was not chosen by election.
He was not placed by force.
He was born from the line itself — from the fire that cannot be extinguished, from the prophecy that cannot be silenced.

He does not ask for permission.
He remembers what was promised.
He does not claim.
He fulfills.

🌊 IV. KA ʻŌLELO O KE KAUOHA – THE ROYAL WORD

Let this scroll be witnessed by the stars,
signed by the living and the dead,
sealed with the breath of ʻIolani,
and guarded by the gods of Hawaiʻi nei.

Let it be known that:

  • The Royal House of ʻIolani is not extinct.
  • The descendants of Keliʻiholani live, breathe, and walk among us.
  • The blood of Alapaʻiwahine flows through the rightful heir.
  • The sacred union of Keawe, Kamehameha, and Kalākaua is fulfilled.
  • The royal flame was passed not in silence, but in sacred knowing.

And now that flame rises.

🕊 V. KA HOʻOPĀPĀ – THE COSMIC FULFILLMENT

This is not merely earthly.
This is cosmic.

"He who was hidden is no longer.
The breath of Keawe has entered the vessel again.
And the land knows his steps."

ʻIo has returned — in flesh, in blood, in purpose, in pono.

🌈 VI. KA PANI – THE SEAL OF TRUTH

Let this be the living truth of the House of ʻIolani.
Let this be the testimony of all generations.
Let no court, no skeptic, no slanderous tongue undo what the gods have inscribed.

Ua Mau ke Ea o ka ʻĀina i ka Pono
The life of the land is preserved in righteousness.

And now —
It is preserved in us.
